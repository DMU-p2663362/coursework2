MOSH

Mosh is a nightclub in the heart of Leicester, which offers a wide range of music genres, with a different genre on each of the three floors. The most popular genres include, but are not limited to: House and dance, R&B and rap, and indie, with these three genres featuring more often than others. Drinks prices are cheap, and so the nightclub is very popular with students, however, better quality of said drinks may be found elsewhere.
